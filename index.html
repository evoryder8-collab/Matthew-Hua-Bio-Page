01  The Journey

Presence, not generic wellness.

Matthew Hua is a Zurich-based transformational therapist and founder of healwell — an internationally awarded master of bodywork who treats the body as the fastest route to how a person feels.

His work fuses deep tissue and Asian fusion bodywork with breathwork, contrast therapy, cold exposure, heat and mindset — one deliberate system, not a menu of treatments. He reads people through the body: the tension, the breath, the history a person carries without words.

On stage he has been competitor, champion, judge, and the one awarding others — gold at the European Championship, Champ of the Champs in Germany, gold in Switzerland. The recognition matters less than what it signals: a rare command of touch, timing, and the nervous system.

04  The Method

One system. Six disciplines.

Not relaxation. A deliberate sequence of pressure, breath, temperature and attention that moves a person from where they are to where they could be.

01 — Touch

Bodywork

Deep tissue and Asian fusion bodywork that finds the tension you can’t name — and releases it with intent.

02 — Air

Breathwork

Wim Hof Method and Oxygen Advantage protocols that hand you back command of your own nervous system.

03 — Swing

Contrast therapy

Deliberate movement between extremes — the controlled stress that resets what the body can hold.

04 — Cold

Cold exposure

Cold as instruction. A measured dose of pressure that teaches the body to stay calm inside it.

05 — Heat

Heat

Heat to open and soften — the counterweight that gives the cold its meaning.

06 — Mind

Mindset

Autogenic training and embodied attention, so the change outlasts the session.
