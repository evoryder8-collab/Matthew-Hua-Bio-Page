Release Cutter

The therapist's hand, turned into a living electrode.

A rotating spiral current runs from fingertip to fascia, threading the path of least resistance through tissue that fixed electrodes can never read. The hand that finds the restriction is the same hand that releases it.

Dense fascia and stagnant lymph release under intelligent current, not force.

Mechanism & technology +

The Release Cutter delivers a proprietary waveform, Eddy Current Muscle Stimulation (ECMS), marketed as a 4D current. Unlike conventional TENS or EMS, which push a flat current along a fixed line between stationary pads, ECMS generates a rotating spiral electric field inside the tissue.

4D rotating currentConductive coupling glovesDouble-glove protocolPhase-shifted dual outputDual pneumatic foot switchesRelease Cutter Cream
  • Fascia is anisotropic. Its conductivity changes with collagen fiber direction, so a rotating vector reaches tissue from multiple angles.
  • The rotating field can reduce the sharp sting of traditional EMS and reduce nerve accommodation, so intensity need not constantly escalate.
  • The intended experience is comfortable, stable, deep stimulation through the myofascia.
